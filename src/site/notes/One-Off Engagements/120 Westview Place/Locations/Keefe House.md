# Keefe House

----
## Name

Keefe House
Herbert House
120 Westview Place

## Summary
Classic spooky American haunted house. Victorian, old pipes, rusty old things in the basement and a fireplace. It's a huge house with poor lighting, lots of junk left behind by previous owners, and cobwebs.
